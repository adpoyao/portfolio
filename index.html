about

Eddie Po Yao

The ability to create something out of nothing have since forever fascinated me. This affinity for creation is what eventually set me off on a path of becoming a full-stack web developer. Being a web developer is almost as if I am casting magic with my fingertips to make this (in the tone of Miranda Priestly) ...stuff exist, and within that context is where I find so much passion for what I do.

Sometimes, I go places. Check out my photos page for some casual photography of memorable locations I've passed by in these recent years.

More... Eddie Po Yao

This webpage is made entirely of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, with help from JQuery and Lightbox.

My other languages are Node.js, Express, MongoDB, PostgresQL, React/Redux, Passport, Mocha/Chai, Travis CI, Heroku and Netlify. It is continuing to grow.
