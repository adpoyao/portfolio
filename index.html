about

Eddie Po Yao

The ability to create something out of nothing have since forever fascinated me. This affinity for creation is what eventually set me off on a path of becoming a full-stack web developer. Being a web developer is almost as if I am casting magic with my fingertips to make this (in the tone of Miranda Priestly) ...stuff exist, and within that context is where I find so much passion for what I do.

As a hobby, I go places. Check out my photos page for some casual pictures of places I've backpacked to in the recent years. I go around teaching yoga and qigong too.

More... Eddie Po Yao

This webpage is made entirely of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and with a little help from JQuery and Lightbox.

I also fiddle in: Node.js, Express, React/Redux, React Native, MongoDB, PostgresQL, REST API, Passport Authentications and CICD.

Training wise, I play Codewars mainly for inspirational moments when I chuckle out of admiration at some people's solutions which are more clever than mine.
